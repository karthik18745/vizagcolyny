History of the Place

Vizag Colony, situated near Nagarjuna Sagar Dam, is known for its scenic beauty and tranquil environment. The colony is a popular destination for visitors looking to enjoy the serene landscapes around the dam. The area around the dam has historical significance, as it was built in the 1960s to provide irrigation and drinking water to the region. The dam and its surrounding areas are rich in natural beauty and offer a glimpse into the region's development over the decades.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Vizag Colony is from November to March. During these months, the weather is cooler and more pleasant, making it ideal for sightseeing and enjoying outdoor activities around Nagarjuna Sagar Dam.

Nearby Places to Visit
  • Nagarjuna Sagar Dam: One of the largest dams in India, offering scenic views and recreational activities.
  • Acharya Nagarjuna University: A prominent university located nearby, known for its academic and research facilities.
  • Bhagvan Mahavir Park: A well-maintained park offering a peaceful environment and picnic spots.
  • Ethipothala Falls: A beautiful waterfall located a short drive from Nagarjuna Sagar Dam, ideal for nature lovers.
  • Guntur: A city located about 70 km away, known for its historical and cultural sites.
Travel Routes

To reach Vizag Colony near Nagarjuna Sagar Dam, use the following routes:

  • From Hyderabad Airport: The colony is approximately 150 km away. You can hire a cab or take a bus towards Nagarjuna Sagar.
  • From Hyderabad Railway Station: The colony is about 140 km away. Cabs and local buses are available for travel.
  • From Vijayawada: The colony is around 110 km away. You can reach there by bus or hire a taxi.
